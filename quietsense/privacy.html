Privacy Policy for QuietSense

Effective date: May 20, 2026

QuietSense is designed to help users understand everyday sound levels and possible transmission risk in shared homes while keeping audio handling simple and privacy-conscious.

Information the app uses

  • Microphone input: QuietSense uses microphone input to estimate sound level. Raw audio is not saved by default, and the app does not transcribe speech.
  • Environment and activity choices: The app stores the environment and activity you choose so it can estimate risk and remember your setup.
  • Local history: The app may store sound-level summaries, risk states, activity summaries, and calibration settings on your device.
  • Purchases: Subscription status is handled through Apple's StoreKit and App Store systems.

What we do not collect

QuietSense does not require an account, does not use speech transcription, and does not ask for location data for the core app experience.

How information is used

Information is used to provide monitoring, history, calibration, tips, and subscription access inside QuietSense.
