QuietSense Support

Need help with QuietSense, subscriptions, microphone permission, calibration, or sound history? Send a support request and include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Email support: asdftqtq+quietsense@gmail.com

Common notes

  • QuietSense estimates sound level and transmission risk. It is not a legal sound meter.
  • The app cannot determine exact direction from one microphone, such as upstairs versus next door.
  • For subscription questions, you can also manage purchases in your Apple Account subscription settings.
