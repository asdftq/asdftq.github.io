About Jet Lag Bye

Jet Lag Bye is your personal jet lag prevention companion. Using proven scientific methods and your specific travel details, we create customized plans to help you adjust your circadian rhythm before, during, and after your trip.

Simply enter your departure and arrival locations with dates, and receive a day-by-day plan including sleep schedules, light exposure timing, caffeine recommendations, and optional melatonin suggestions.

Key Features

  • Personalized Plans: Custom jet lag prevention strategies based on your specific travel itinerary and timezone changes
  • Sleep Schedule Adjustment: Gradual pre-trip sleep adjustments to minimize jet lag impact
  • Light Exposure Guidance: Precise timing for seeking or avoiding bright light to reset your body clock
  • Smart Caffeine Strategy: Recommendations for when to consume or avoid caffeine for optimal adjustment
  • Round Trip Support: Separate plans for outbound and return journeys to keep you adjusted throughout your trip
  • Notification Reminders: Optional alerts for sleep times, light exposure, and other recommendations
  • Travel History: Save and revisit your past jet lag plans for reference
  • Dark Mode Support: Easy on the eyes interface that adapts to your device settings

How It Works

  1. Enter Your Trip: Input your departure and arrival cities with travel dates
  2. Set Your Sleep Schedule: Tell us your normal bedtime and wake time
  3. Get Your Plan: Receive a personalized day-by-day jet lag prevention plan
  4. Follow Recommendations: Adjust your sleep, light exposure, and caffeine intake as suggested
  5. Arrive Refreshed: Minimize jet lag and enjoy your trip from day one!
